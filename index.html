Need some IT help?

My name is Noah S. Roberts. I am a student at the University of Montana with a passion for technology. I work in UM IT, but I also work with independent clients on the side.

I offer a variety of services:

  • New device setup assistance
  • Home Internet setup and troubleshooting
  • TV setup and troubleshooting
  • Computer troubleshooting, diagnosis, and repair
  • And more!

Concerning smart phones and computers, I am an Apple-first technician. However, I am still comfortable and knowledgeable with Windows and, to a lesser degree, Android.

Many issues experienced by users are related to the software. This means that the physical device (i.e. phone, laptop, etc.) is not often the culprit. If an issue is due to a problem with the physical device, I will do my best to address it.

I only conduct in-home visits at this time. If your device requires more love and care to get it in tip-top shape, I may request to conduct my repairs offsite where I have more space and more tools to do so.

Information about my rates and services offered can be found on the Services & Rates page. If you have any questions or concerns regarding payment or repair timelines, please visit the Frequently Asked Questions page or me a message. My contact information can be found here.
